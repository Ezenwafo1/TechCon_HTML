History of TechCon

TechCon has a rich history of facilitating conversations around technological advancements and their impact on society.

Mission Of TechCon

The mission of the conference is to provide a forum for engaging with the people and leaders building our future and for celebrating the growth of tech hubs like Chicago as destinations for innovation.

Past Speakers

Over the years, TechCon has featured a diverse lineup of speakers who are leaders in their respective fields. Notable past speakers include:

  • Marti Konstant: A workplace futurist and best-selling author, she delivered a keynote on career agility, emphasizing the importance of embracing disruption and change in one's career.
  • Deirdre Hanford: CEO of Natcast.org, she presented a keynote titled "Future Forward: How R&D will Continue to Propel the Semiconductor Industry," inspiring attendees with insights into the future of research and development.
  • Eitan Shay: Chief Product Officer at DarioHealth, he discussed "Velocity as a Virtue," highlighting how accelerating product development can lead to quicker user feedback and innovation

Projects

MindBridge – Mental Health Support Platform

A responsive web app that connects users with licensed mental health professionals via chat and video sessions. Features include anonymous messaging, emergency support, and a blog offering coping strategies. Inspired by your passion to serve emotionally vulnerable users without needing physical visits. Tools: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Firebase

AfroLens – Africa-Centric Storytelling Platform

A multimedia site that publishes spoken word pieces, historical articles, and animated short stories about African identity, struggle, and resilience. Visitors can listen, read, and comment on each piece. Built to combat cultural erasure and celebrate African voices. Tools: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Tailwind CSS, Markdown integration

CodePulse – Developer Motivation Dashboard

A minimalist, personal dashboard for developers filled with motivational quotes, embedded tweets, learning milestones, and progress tracking. The dashboard updates dynamically and encourages consistency and mental well-being while learning to code. Tools: JavaScript, Local Storage, Twitter API, Bootstrap
